School Moto


The School Motto is enshrined in the Sanskrit expression "Gyan- Bhanur - Bhastam" meaning "May the sun of knowledge shine". The sun knows no discrimination and engulfs the entire world with light & warmth. Our effort are directed to help our students to enlighten them with knowledge and wisdom and forge ahead providing them with the right opportunities and ideal environment for the development of their personality.
We endeavor to instill in the students a sense of responsibility & initiative to become useful member of the society so that they can competently play their vital role in the nation building process. Our logo is in synchronous with our motto.
