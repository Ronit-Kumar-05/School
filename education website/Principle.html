Message From The Principal


Dear Parents,
In the uncertain times of the 2020-2021 school year, there are two things we know for certain, one, this year had been different than any "Academic Session" in the history of Education World and secondly, our students, parents, teachers and staff can count on St. Joseph school Although Covid 19 has changed the human perception in many ways, as the present generation never ever thought to come across any such crisis, so the question of having any ready plan to tackle the situation in which the present human race found themselves since March 2020 doesn't arise. The above crisis moulded believes, thought and practices of present day human race across the globe. So, education world not being exception and therefore school may have looked different this year with online learning and, hopefully, at some point returning to an in-built hybrid model. Yet, what never changed, is our commitment of serving our students, families, and community with care, compassion, and dedication. We call our community the "SJS Family" because we want our school family to be an extension of your family. We want to serve you in times of joy, happiness, and triumph, and also in times of challenge, grief, and struggle. Academic learning and growth are our bottom line, yet we also assert that educating students means being attentive to their "cognitive development" and taking care of their social and emotional well-being. Our teachers and staff left no stone unturned to serve their students and we love to be a part of their learning and growth during these crucial years of development. We are fortunate to have parents like you in our school and contributing to our positive school culture, although it is mostly online this year. Parents and community are a big part of who we are, and we want you to help us tell our story.

St. Joseph
