Dear Parents,
An educational institute is not just about bricks, mortar and concrete but about building character, enriching minds and experience that last a life time. Life is not a set of instructions but is a series of experiences and continual learning process. This is where St Joseph School School steps in to make a difference where learning is not just a series of instructions but a passion which goes beyond books, beyond instructions, beyond learning horizons. Education is the right of each and every child irrespective of caste, creed and religion. Education survives and emerges where everything else fails. It is a priceless gift of wisdom a gift more precious than any materialistic gain. The school is committed to growth, innovation and maintaining high standards of educational programmes blended with Indian cultural heritage. Here we encourage our students to dream beyond horizon also we not only impart quality education but also groom our children into socially interactive, mentally agile and empathetic individuals also. It is with this vision that St Joseph School School, Rewari has forged ahead with a mission to hone and integrate the personality of every student under its fold. It is our aim to help all students achieve a level of excellence in their chosen fields and attain goals hitherto unexplored.

St. Joseph
