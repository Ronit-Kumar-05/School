Who We Are


ST. JOSEPH SCHOOL was established in year 2011 and It runs under the contribution of ‘Vivek educational and welfare society’ at vaishali district Registered under the society registration Act 21, of 1860 with the Govt of Bihar having its Regd.No.-0018/2013-14. To cultivate the knowledge of every people at rural area, the St. Joseph School has devoted time to lit the light of education. The establishment of St. Joseph School aim is the all around development of the students to understand and live their faith and to impart to all students with sound education by forming habits of study, discipline, self reliance during the year of their studies, so as to make them worth. These parameters are known under the continuous and comprehansive side evaluation process under CCE Curriculum recently launched by the CBSE for its school is devoted to generate human resource of caliber to realize our basis philosphy on which we stand, That is. Location It is located near mahua town P.S.- Mahua , Dist : Vaishali in Bihar. The city is well connected by road transport facilities through city Railway station and other Bus stands. It is aimed at benifit aspirations of those who are residents of Nothern part of Hajipur city and largely suburban area of the city and urban area of remote village where earlier there was on educational institution as such. Therefore it is ideally located to fulfill the educational aspiration of society
